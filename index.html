About Us


Bene Vale means 'Well worth it' in Italian. Bene Vale is an ode to the Italian love affair with food and dining... perhaps the only way to dine! With the whole of Italy as his inspiration and larder, Chef personally handpicks the finest produce of its soil. Then, brilliantly reinventing tradition, he transforms these luscious ingredients into contemporary works of art. Bene Vale is Italian food-craft at its most thoughtful and original and gastronomically refined. It is also a return to cherished basics: the conscious, unhurried enjoyment of flavours; the hospitality that makes you feel right at home; and the enduring memories that come from a truly great meal and a special experience. At Bene Vale every ingredient is selected for seasonality, sustainability, quality and flavor. We explore contemporary ways of paying homage to our beloved Italy's timeless flavor profiles and combinations with our personal touch in every dish. We deliver an attention to detail and service which is unique to the Bene Vale experience. Uncomplicated clean, honest flavours are the essence of our inspiration and passion for food.
